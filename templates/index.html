How to use SlideCraft Lite

Workflow

  1. Upload a PPTX (or drag it onto the page).
  2. Remove Logo erases the NotebookLM watermark from every slide.
  3. Watermark adds a text or image overlay (CONFIDENTIAL, your logo, etc.) to the current slide or all slides.
  4. Duplicate / Delete tweak the deck structure. Drag thumbnails to reorder.
  5. Save creates a checkpoint you can roll back to.
  6. Export downloads the cleaned deck as PPTX or PDF; PNG for the current slide only.
  7. Reset restores the original uploaded slides.

Bulk processing

The Bulk button accepts up to 10 PPTX files totalling up to 300 MB. Logos are removed from every slide and a ZIP of cleaned PPTX files is downloaded.

Tips

  • Erase Box: click the button, then drag any rectangle on the slide. The selected area is replaced by tiling the row of pixels just above it — same trick as the logo remover, but anywhere you want.
  • Per-session storage: your decks are isolated from other users by a cookie. Clear your cookies and you'll get a fresh workspace. Sessions older than 24 hours are auto-cleaned.
  • Recent decks: the last 5 uploads stay one click away in the Recent menu.
